It's The Bridge Homepage!

This site is an interactive tool designed to BRIDGE the gap between our precious children and their incarcerated parents, guardians, or loved ones by giving them better access to what, when and how their children are learning.

Tools included on this site provide the parent on the other end of the bridge with real-time access to:

  1. Assignments scores and due dates.
  2. Access to information about missing/incomplete/failed/assignments, as well notification of extra credit opportunities.
  3. Accumulative grades.

Site users will also be provided with:

  1. The ability communicate with administrators from your student's school by messaging via our messenger feature.
  2. Access to our library of educational, reading and videos meant to be used as talk points for you and your child.

Bridging the gap starts with ParentView here !
